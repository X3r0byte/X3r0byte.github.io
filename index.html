A little about me


My interest in computers is long-standing. As a kid, I would alter game code, tinker with robotics, and pour through my game development book in C++. After some years of self search, I went back to academia at the age of 26 to start and finish a Bachelor's in Computer Science.

Today, I am working as a Software Engineer for a health tech company which aims to simplify the exchange of PHI throughout the healthcare system. I hope to make an impact on this system, as it is primed for opportunity!

Currently, I am:

  • learning Azure Cloud deployment
  • developing pipeline CI / CD
  • building a text analysis SaaS
  • learning how to integrate ML & AI
  • career planning and promoting self development
  • looking forward to any opportunity that comes my way

ASTR
Intro

ASTR - a mashup of Asteroids and Lander - is a Unity project. The core of the game is my Gravitizer script, which adds a physics challenge to landing on celestials like Lander to the gameplay of Asteroids.

Notes

This is a work in progress! Currently, gameplay is only shooting asteroids for loot pickups and playing with the gravity effect. I am working on landing, mining, and multiplayer, as well as a purpose or story.

Controls
  • Movement: WASD or L/R softkeys
  • Shoot: click or large softkey
  • Mine: small softkey (not implemented yet)
